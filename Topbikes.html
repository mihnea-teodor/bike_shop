Last year was, like usual, a good year for new bikes.Once accepted, the new wheel size 650B, a new series of wonderfull forms had arrived - the producers needed bikes for this particular segment so they created some brand new models, that are really atractive.

How did we pick them?

It was a long and complicated mathematical process, which was debated really intense.Remeber, this is not a tehnical test, or in any way a mechanical view - this is just a simple recognition to those bike that could cause you a headache just beacuse they look so good, you would die to take it for a spin.

This is not in a precise order, just our top 5 favorite bikes.

1.Yeti SB75

The Super Bike Yeti is on the market for two years now, but we are definitely sure it has reach the stars by now.The fact that Yeti offers the model in yellow, compensates the fact that they will most definitely bring a carbon version on the market soon.

2.Rocky Mountain Altitude Rally Edition

Now thats a good looking bike...It has been added more brutality to the bikes lines, but in a pleasent way.The frameworks, tires and the suspension had been modified to new wider ones.Just a touch of phosphorescen rounds up this creation.

3.Santa Cruz 5010 C

If there was a prize for the most "fancy" bike, then, clearly the 5010 C would win with brio.Named 'Solo' at first,Santa Cruz recevied some legal letters from a prducer with a product that had the same name.But, no matter what name, the sweet lines of the carbon fiber, the 650B wheels and the introduction made by Steve Peat transformed this into a strong tentation for any rider.

4.Stevie's Chainsaw Massacre

No doubt, this was one of the most discussed bike's of 2013.The Stevie Smith Chainsaw Massacre model, was a special edition for the World Championship Pietermaritzburg.This was the fastest bike during the practice.If DH represents Formula 1 of mountain bike, then this special edition Dave Weagle was in the top of the tops.

5.Cotic Rocket

If the Santa Cruz 5010 C model is the ode of country trails from California, the the Cotic Rocket is a punk rock chore wich brings glory to the mercyless woods of Great Britain.The wheels have a "normal" size, the frame is made from steel made by Reynolds 853 and the race 150mm transforms this bike into one mad monster.
